About Mani Traders

Your trusted partner for seamless financial solutions in Malaysia

Our Story

Delivering excellence in money exchange and remittance since 2008

Who We Are

Mani Traders Sdn Bhd (MTSB) was incorporated in 2008 in the state of Penang, serving primarily retail customers in the northern region of Malaysia. After more than a decade, in August 2020, MTSB relocated to Selangor House in the Masjid India area, and then in September 2022 to No. 1006, Selangor Mansion, Jalan Masjid India.

Currently, we are one of the leaders in money exchange services, licensed under Money Service Business by Bank Negara Malaysia ("BNM").

Our Mission

We are one of the best Money Services Partners in Malaysia, enabling customers to exchange and transfer money anytime and anywhere. To date, we have 2 outlets in KL.

With 30 currencies, we offer the best and most competitive rates for all our currencies to our valuable customers, assisted by a team of experienced and dedicated MT members that are exceptionally reliable with excellent services.

Our dedicated team is committed to your financial success.
